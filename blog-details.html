How to validate your SaaS idea in under 30 days

Product • 6 min read • Updated Nov 2025

Launching software is a race against time. Startup Landing was designed so that teams can validate ideas faster than ever—without waiting on engineering resources or custom tooling.

1. Craft a focused landing page

Start with a single narrative. Highlight a clear value proposition, outline benefits, and include social proof. Use our hero, features, and testimonial sections to frame the story.

2. Capture qualified leads

Pair your pitch with a contact form or waitlist signup. Use microcopy near the CTA to explain what subscribers will receive after signing up.

Tip: Use the form-handler.js script to connect to Formspree, Zapier, or your own endpoint.

3. Iterate with data

Monitor heatmaps, scroll depth, and CTA clicks. Update headlines, swap sections, and retest quickly.
